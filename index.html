About me

After working in industry as an Industrial Engineer, I realized that I wanted to do something different. I spoke with a number of friends about my situation and came to the conclusion that I wanted to become a data scientist. I worked with a friend in the industry on a few side projects to see if this was the type of work I wanted to do, and I have to say I enjoyed it a lot.

I am currently pursuing a MS in Business Analytics and Information Management at Purdue University with a graduation date of May 2023. I have enjoyed working on linear optimization, web scraping, and machine learning problems in my time at Purdue. I am interested in remote positions with a start date in June 2023.
